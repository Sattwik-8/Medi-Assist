How It Works

From Symptoms to
Guidance in Seconds

No forms, no appointments, no jargon. Just type what you're feeling — our AI does the rest.

1

Describe Symptoms

Type your symptoms naturally — "I have fever, headache and nausea" — in English, Hindi, or Bengali.

2

AI Analysis

Our SVC model converts your input into a weighted symptom vector and runs a real-time prediction.

3

Get a Full Report

Receive your predicted condition with a confidence score, description, medicines, diet, and precautions.

4

Take Action

Decide with confidence — whether to rest, visit a pharmacy, or consult a doctor urgently.
