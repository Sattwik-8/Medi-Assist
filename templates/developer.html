Project Contributions

How the Work Came Together

1

AI & Machine Learning

The team trained and integrated a symptom-based disease prediction model, added confidence scoring, severity labels, and safety notes so the output feels clear and responsible.

2

Full-Stack Application

Frontend and backend work were combined into a Flask application with responsive pages, chatbot interaction, prediction APIs, contact flow, downloadable reports, and deployment-ready configuration.

3

Data, QA & Content

Dataset preparation, quality checks, content writing, and testing shaped the reliability of the experience, from symptom suggestions to medicines, precautions, diet, and workout guidance.

4

NLP & Research

Natural-language handling and research support improved how MediBot understands user messages, responds to general questions, and guides users toward safer health decisions.

5

Safety First

The app includes medical disclaimers, emergency message detection, and guidance to consult qualified professionals before making treatment decisions.
