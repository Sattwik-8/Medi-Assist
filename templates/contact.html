FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Medi-Assist a real medical service? ▾
No. Medi-Assist is an AI-powered educational and informational tool. It is not a licensed medical service, and its predictions are not a diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.
How accurate is the disease prediction? ▾
Our SVC model achieves 95%+ accuracy on the training and test datasets. However, accuracy varies with real-world inputs. The system works best when multiple clear symptoms are provided. Always treat predictions as informational, not definitive.
Does Medi-Assist store my symptom data? ▾
Medi-Assist does not store or log your symptom queries. Each prediction is processed in real-time and discarded. No personal health data is retained on our servers.
Can I use Medi-Assist without internet? ▾
The ML model itself requires no external API calls — it runs entirely on the server. You do need internet access to reach the web app, but the prediction engine has zero external API dependency once the app is running.
How can I collaborate or contribute? ▾
We welcome contributions — whether it's expanding the symptom database, improving the ML model, translating to more languages, or providing clinical validation feedback. Use the contact form above to get in touch with details about your proposal.
🚨

Experiencing a Medical Emergency?

Do not use Medi-Assist in an emergency. If you or someone nearby is in immediate danger or experiencing severe symptoms such as chest pain, difficulty breathing, loss of consciousness, or heavy bleeding — call emergency services immediately.
