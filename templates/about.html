Our Mission

Built for the Billion Without Easy Access to Healthcare

India has one doctor for every 1,500 people. Rural areas are far worse. Medi-Assist exists to bridge that gap.

Why We Built This

Medi-Assist was born from a simple but powerful belief — that quality health information should be accessible to everyone, regardless of where they live or what they can afford. Millions of people across India self-medicate, ignore warning symptoms, or wait far too long before seeking help — not out of negligence, but because access to timely medical guidance simply isn't there.

We built Medi-Assist to be that first line of guidance. A smart, offline-capable tool that listens to your symptoms in plain language, thinks like a trained clinician, and delivers a clear, actionable health report in seconds — no appointment needed, no internet required, no medical jargon to decode. Just answers, when and where you need them.

For healthcare professionals, it serves as a rapid differential diagnosis aid. For patients, it's a trusted companion that empowers informed decisions. For students, it's a live demonstration of AI in medicine.

What We Offer

A Complete Health Guidance Platform

One tool, six types of clinical guidance — all derived from a single symptom description.

🦠

Disease Prediction

Our SVC classifier analyzes your symptom vector and returns the top-3 most probable diagnoses with confidence scores and severity indicators.

💊

Medication Overview

Understand which medications are commonly prescribed for your condition — so your conversation with a pharmacist or doctor is informed, not blind.

⚠️

Precautions & Safety

Know exactly what to do and what to avoid after a potential diagnosis — reducing the risk of worsening a condition through common mistakes.

🥗

Dietary Guidance

Condition-specific food recommendations to support recovery. Know what your body needs and what it should avoid during illness.

🏃

Exercise Recommendations

Safe physical activity guidance tailored to your predicted condition — because rest and movement both matter, in the right balance.

💬

Multilingual MediBot

Chat naturally in English, Hindi, or Bengali. MediBot understands colloquial symptom descriptions and responds in your chosen language.

How It Works

Four Steps from Symptoms to Clarity

Medi-Assist is designed to feel effortless — describe what you feel, and receive a structured health report within seconds.

Try it now →
1

Describe Your Symptoms

Type your symptoms in plain language — "I have fever, headache, and nausea since yesterday" — in English, Hindi, or Bengali. No medical terminology required.

2

AI Analyzes the Pattern

Our trained Support Vector Classifier converts your symptoms into a severity-weighted vector and runs a real-time prediction across 41 disease classes.

3

Receive a Full Health Report

Get a ranked differential diagnosis with confidence scores, disease description, medications, precautions, dietary advice, and safe workout guidance.

4

Take Informed Action

Use your report to decide: monitor at home, visit a pharmacy, or see a doctor urgently. Severity indicators guide the urgency of your next step.
